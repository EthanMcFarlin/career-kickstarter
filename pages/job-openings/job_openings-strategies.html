In-Depth Analysis of Unemployment Strategies

The unemployment process has the reputation of cementing weeks or months of tribulation, incessantly jumping from one interview to the next and leaving both applicants and employers endlessly tired.

Luckily, the reality of the situation is far less grim. The unemployment process, albeit daunting, can actually serve as an oportunity to boost your career to new heights. Our thorough analysis of this subject is designed to help you make the best of your circumstances.

With clearly defined goals, you should be ready to prepare a schedule. The vitality of this step cannot be understated. WIthout a schedule, you may find yourself falling into the habit repeated procrastination and the process in its entirety may stretch out a few additional weeks.

Phone Etiquette:

  • Verify that your voicemail box is not full

Email Etiquette:

  • Send a professionally titled email address
  • Add a professional email signature for extra flare
  • Use a grammar checker such as Grammarly to catch any spelling or punctuation mistakes
